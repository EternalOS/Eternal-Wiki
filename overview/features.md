## Reserved Battery (Coming Soon!)

Get amazing battery life with **Reserved Battery!** When Reserved Battery is Enabled the Last 5% of Battery life is used to keep to phone alive in the most difficult situations, we aim to let the last 5% of the battery to last upto 3 hours so in Emergency situations the user can still stay connected to the world, The feature works by killing all background applications and only allow phone, camera (with limited access), Enable/Disable GPS, turn off Mobile Data if Wifi is available, And keep messaging apps accessible...
